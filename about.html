about me

I am an aspiring designer studying Human Computer Interaction at UC San Diego. I interned this past summer at Wunderman, a WPP advertising agency, as a creative intern and was first exposed to user experience and interaction concepts during this time. I first dabbled in the design field as a freelance graphic designer but now I am excited to explore the application of design through the lens of user experience. Therefore, I am currently seeking user experience and human centered design focused internships. I believe that there are so many innovative ideas that can truly impact the world through this field and I remain excited for all the future possibilities.
